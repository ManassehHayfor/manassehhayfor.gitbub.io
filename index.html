About Me

Hello! I'm Manasseh Hayford Mensah, a data analyst with a passion for uncovering the stories hidden within numbers. My journey began at the warehouse floor of DP World, where I discovered the thrill of solving operational mysteries through data.

As a Business Administration student at the University of Ghana, I combined academic knowledge with hands-on experience. At DP World, I developed data reconciliation systems that reduced stock shortages by 40%—an achievement that solidified my passion for analytics.

The "Detective Work" Thrill

Uncovering hidden stories in data feels like solving puzzles. For example:

  • Finding why customer churn spiked after a "successful" product update
  • Spotting a $500K cost-saving opportunity in a client's supply chain log

That "aha!" moment? Addictive. It's what drives my passion for data analytics.

I later pursued formal training through ALX's Data Analytics program, mastering Python, SQL, Power BI, and other essential tools. Today, I bridge the gap between operational experience and technical expertise to deliver insights that drive real business impact.
